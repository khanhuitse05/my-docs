# 📱 App Release Checklist (For Project Owners)

This guide will help you set up your Apple and Google developer accounts and grant access to our development team so we can publish your app.

---

## ✅ What you need before starting

Please prepare the following:

- Company legal name (for Organization account)
- Company email (recommended, not personal email)
- Phone number (for verification)
- Credit card:
  - Apple: $99/year
  - Google: $25 one-time
- Developer emails (we will provide)

---

## 🏢 Choose Account Type

### 1. Individual

- For personal apps
- Registered under your personal name

### 2. Organization (Recommended ✅)

- For companies/businesses
- App will be published under your company name
- Requires:
  - Legal company entity
  - D-U-N-S number: <https://developer.apple.com/enroll/duns-lookup/#!/search>

👉 **Recommendation:**

- If you are a company → choose **Organization**

---

## 🍎 Apple Developer Account Setup

### Step 1: Register Account

1. Go to: <https://developer.apple.com/programs/enroll/>
2. Sign in with your Apple ID
3. Complete registration
4. Pay $99/year
5. Wait for approval (usually 1–3 business days)

---

### Step 2: Add Our Development Team

1. Go to: <https://appstoreconnect.apple.com>
2. Click **Users and Access**
3. Click **+ Add User**
4. Enter the developer email (we will provide)
5. Set role:
   - **App Manager** (recommended)
   - or **Admin**

---

### Step 3: Setup Payment & Tax

- Add your banking and tax information
- This is required to receive payments from the App Store

---

## 🤖 Google Play Developer Account Setup

### Step 1: Register Account

1. Go to: <https://play.google.com/console/>
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Pay $25 one-time fee
4. Complete identity verification
5. Fill in your developer profile

---

### Step 2: Add Our Development Team

1. Go to Google Play Console
2. Navigate to **Users and Permissions**
3. Click **Invite New Users**
4. Enter developer email (we will provide)
5. Assign role:
   - **Admin** or
   - **Release Manager**

---

### Step 3: Setup Payments Profile

- Create or select a payments profile
- Required if your app includes paid features

---

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

### Issue: Cannot verify phone number (Google Play)

Try the following:

- Use international format: +[country code][number]
  - Example: +14155552671
- Try SMS or voice call
- Wait 24–72 hours before retry
- If still not working, contact support:
  <https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/gethelp>

### Issue: Copyrighted/Trademarked material policy (scan apps)

Google's policies prohibit apps from facilitating unauthorized use of copyrighted or trademarked material. For a scan app with unrestricted user input:

**Risks:**


- Users might scan copyrighted text (e.g., from books or articles)
- They could scan branded QR codes or logos (e.g., from Coca-Cola, Nike), which are protected by trademark law
- The app might display, store, or process this scanned content, implicating you as the developer if it's misused

**What to do:** Add a disclaimer to both your app and Play Store listing. Example:

> [App Name] is a general-purpose scanning tool. It does not endorse or facilitate the unauthorized use of copyrighted or trademarked content. Users are solely responsible for obtaining permission from rights holders for any scanned material.
